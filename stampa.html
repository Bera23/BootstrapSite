O nama

Comics Store je otvorena 2018. I prva je prodavnica stripova koja je otvorena u Srbiji.
Za svoje kupce spremili smo najnovije stripove iz producijskih kuća kao što su Marvel, DC Comics, the New Age of DC Heroes, Marvel Legacy, DC Rebirth, Image Comics, i mnogi drugi! Vaše omiljene superheroje možete naći ovde, Batman, Superman, Wonder Woman, Supergirl, Spider-Man, the Avengers, the Flash, Captain America, the X-Men, Green Lantern, Wolverine, Black Widow, Thor, Daredevil, Dr. Strange, Jessica Jones, Iron Fist, the Defenders, Captain Marvel, Ms. Marvel, the Punisher, Green Arrow, the Incredible Hulk, Iron Man, Hellboy, Fantastic Four, the Walking Dead, iZombie, Luke Cage, Kingsman, Suicide Squad, Harley Quinn, i drugi!
